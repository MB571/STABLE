Wellness Tool — Important Notice

STABLE is a free personal wellness tool, not a medical device. It has not been approved or evaluated by the FDA, CE, or any regulatory body, and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. Do not use as a substitute for prescribed medication or neurological care.

Full disclaimer ▾
STABLE should not be used by people with cochlear implants, cardiac pacemakers, or epilepsy, or by anyone with a sensitivity to audio or vibration stimulation. Individual responses to frequency stimulation vary significantly. If you experience dizziness, increased discomfort, or worsening symptoms at any point, stop immediately. Always consult your neurologist before beginning any new therapeutic practice. The audio channels in STABLE are based on published research in vibrotactile stimulation and neural entrainment, but their application via bone conduction headphones has not been validated in clinical trials.
